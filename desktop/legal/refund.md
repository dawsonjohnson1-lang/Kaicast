## 1. OVERVIEW

This Refund Policy governs refunds for consumer subscription purchases made directly through KaiCast's website or web platform. Purchases made through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store are subject to those platforms' own refund policies; KaiCast cannot issue or override refunds for purchases processed through third-party app stores.

---

## 2. CONSUMER SUBSCRIPTIONS

### 2.1 General Policy
All subscription fees are non-refundable except as expressly stated in this policy. When you cancel a paid subscription, you will retain access to paid features until the end of your current billing period. We do not issue prorated refunds for unused time within a billing period.

### 2.2 Free Trials
If you cancel a free trial before it converts to a paid subscription, you will not be charged. If you do not cancel before the end of the trial period, your subscription will automatically convert to a paid subscription, and the applicable fee will be charged. The standard refund window in Section 2.3 applies from the date of the first charge.

### 2.3 Refund Window
We offer a **7-day refund window** from the date of the initial subscription charge (not applicable to renewals). To request a refund within this window, contact us at dawson@kaicast.com with your account email and the reason for your request. Approved refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

### 2.4 Renewal Charges
Renewal charges are non-refundable. It is your responsibility to cancel your subscription before the renewal date if you do not wish to continue. We will send a renewal reminder email 30 days before annual subscription renewals.

### 2.5 Exceptions — Service Failure
If a service failure directly attributable to KaiCast prevents you from accessing the Service for a continuous period of 72 hours or more during your billing period, you may request a prorated service credit for the affected period. Credits are applied to your account and applied against future charges; they are not refunded in cash. To request a service credit, contact dawson@kaicast.com with documentation of the service failure.

---

## 3. ANNUAL SUBSCRIPTIONS

Annual subscriptions are non-refundable after the 7-day refund window described in Section 2.3. We strongly recommend that you use any available free trial period to evaluate the Service before committing to an annual subscription.

---

## 4. APP STORE PURCHASES

### 4.1 Apple App Store
Subscriptions purchased through the Apple App Store are subject to Apple's refund policy. To request a refund, visit [reportaproblem.apple.com] or contact Apple Support directly. KaiCast cannot process refunds for App Store purchases.

### 4.2 Google Play Store
Subscriptions purchased through the Google Play Store are subject to Google's refund policy. To request a refund, visit [play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions] or contact Google Play Support. KaiCast cannot process refunds for Google Play purchases.

---

## 5. B2B AND ENTERPRISE ACCOUNTS

Refund and credit terms for hotel SaaS (TMHelperAgent), tour operator, and API licensing agreements are governed by the applicable commercial agreement executed between KaiCast and the customer. In the absence of specific terms in the commercial agreement, fees paid are non-refundable.

## 6. CHARGEBACKS

If you initiate a chargeback with your payment provider without first contacting KaiCast to resolve the issue, we reserve the right to suspend or terminate your account pending resolution. We encourage you to contact us at dawson@kaicast.com before disputing a charge — most issues can be resolved quickly.

## 7. HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND

To request a refund under this policy:

1. Email dawson@kaicast.com with the subject line "Refund Request"
2. Include your account email address and the email address on file
3. Include the transaction date and amount
4. Briefly describe the reason for your request

We will respond within 3 business days.
