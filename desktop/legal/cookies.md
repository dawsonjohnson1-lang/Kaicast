## 2. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your device across sessions and store information about your preferences or activity. Cookies can be "session cookies" (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent cookies" (remaining on your device until they expire or you delete them).

In addition to cookies, we may use similar technologies such as pixel tags, web beacons, and local storage objects. For simplicity, this policy refers to all such technologies as "cookies."

---

## 3. TYPES OF COOKIES WE USE

### 3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies
These cookies are essential for the Service to function and cannot be switched off. They are typically set in response to actions you take, such as logging in, maintaining your session, or storing your privacy preferences. Without these cookies, core features of the Service cannot be provided.

Examples of strictly necessary functions include session authentication, security tokens, and cookie consent storage.

### 3.2 Functional Cookies
These cookies enhance the functionality of the Service by remembering your preferences (such as measurement units and favorite dive spots). Disabling them may reduce the quality of your experience but will not prevent you from using the Service.

### 3.3 Analytics Cookies
We use analytics tools — including Firebase Analytics (provided by Google LLC) — to understand how users interact with the Service. Analytics data is collected in aggregate and used to improve platform features and performance. Firebase Analytics may set cookies or use device identifiers in connection with its operation.

For more information about how Google uses data from Firebase Analytics, visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### 3.4 Payment Cookies
When you access checkout or subscription management pages, Stripe, Inc. may set cookies necessary to process your payment securely. These cookies are operational and limited to payment-related pages.

For more information, visit: https://stripe.com/privacy

### 3.5 Marketing Cookies
We do not currently use advertising or marketing cookies. If we introduce them in the future, we will update this policy and obtain your consent where required before setting them.

## 6. YOUR CHOICES

**6.1 Cookie Banner.** When you first access the Service, we will display a notice allowing you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can update your preferences at any time through your account settings or our cookie settings panel.

**6.2 Browser Settings.** You can configure your browser to refuse all or certain cookies. Instructions for common browsers:

- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy
- **Firefox:** Options > Privacy & Security
- **Edge:** Settings > Cookies and site permissions

Note that disabling strictly necessary cookies may prevent you from using core features of the Service.

**6.3 Opt-Out of Google Analytics.** To opt out of Google Analytics tracking, install the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On at: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

**6.4 Do Not Track.** Some browsers transmit "Do Not Track" (DNT) signals. The Service does not currently respond to DNT signals, as there is no established industry standard for compliance. We will update this policy if that changes.

## 7. MOBILE APPLICATIONS

Our mobile application uses equivalent tracking technologies, including device identifiers provided by your operating system. You can limit tracking through your device settings:

- **Android:** Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
