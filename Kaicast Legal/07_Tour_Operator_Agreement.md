## 1. BACKGROUND

KaiCast operates an ocean conditions intelligence platform that aggregates environmental data and firsthand condition reports to generate dive and ocean condition scores for coastal sites. Operator conducts guided dive, snorkel, freedive, or water sports tours and possesses direct, firsthand knowledge of local ocean conditions.

The purpose of this Agreement is to establish a mutual data exchange relationship: Operator contributes real-time condition data based on firsthand observation; KaiCast provides Operator with platform access, exposure, and aggregated conditions data to enhance Operator's service offerings.

---

## 2. DEFINITIONS

"**Operator Listing**" means Operator's business profile on the KaiCast platform, including name, description, contact information, tour offerings, and associated dive sites.

"**Operator-Submitted Data**" means real-time or recent ocean conditions data, site-specific observations, and condition ratings submitted by Operator or Operator's staff to the KaiCast platform based on direct, firsthand observation.

"**Platform**" means KaiCast's consumer and B2B platform, including mobile applications, web applications, and API services.

"**Operating Region**" means the geographic area(s) in which Operator conducts tours, as identified in Schedule A to this Agreement.

---

## 3. OPERATOR OBLIGATIONS

**3.1 Data Submission.** Operator agrees to submit Operator-Submitted Data to the KaiCast platform on a regular basis (recommended: at least once per tour day in which ocean activity occurs) using the method specified by KaiCast (app, API, or web form).

**3.2 Accuracy.** Operator represents and warrants that all Operator-Submitted Data is: (a) based on firsthand observation by Operator or Operator's qualified staff; (b) accurate to the best of Operator's knowledge at the time of submission; and (c) not fabricated, estimated without direct observation, or submitted for sites Operator has not recently visited.

**3.3 Timeliness.** Operator will submit condition data as close to the time of observation as reasonably practicable (same day is strongly preferred).

**3.4 Listing Accuracy.** Operator is responsible for maintaining the accuracy of its Operator Listing. Operator will promptly update KaiCast if business information, tour offerings, or operating areas change.

**3.5 Compliance.** Operator will at all times comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and permits governing its tour operations, including maritime safety regulations, marine protected area access rules, and applicable licensing requirements.

---

## 4. KAICAST OBLIGATIONS

**4.1 Operator Listing.** KaiCast will create and maintain an Operator Listing on the Platform during the Term, subject to Section 8.

**4.2 Platform Access.** KaiCast will provide Operator with access to a designated operator portal or app interface for submitting Operator-Submitted Data.

**4.3 Aggregated Data Reports.** KaiCast will provide Operator with periodic (monthly) aggregated condition reports for sites within Operator's Operating Region to assist Operator in planning and guest communications.

**4.4 Co-Branded Widgets.** KaiCast may, at its discretion, provide Operator with a co-branded conditions widget that Operator may embed on Operator's own website, displaying current Condition Scores for sites in Operator's Operating Region.

## 5. LICENSE GRANTS

**5.1 Operator-Submitted Data License.** Operator hereby grants KaiCast a perpetual, irrevocable, worldwide, royalty-free, sublicensable license to use, reproduce, distribute, modify, and incorporate Operator-Submitted Data into KaiCast's platform, scoring algorithms, aggregated data products, and B2B data offerings. KaiCast may attribute Operator-Submitted Data to Operator on the Platform (e.g., "Reported by [Operator Name]") unless Operator requests anonymous attribution.

**5.2 Listing Content License.** Operator grants KaiCast a license to display Operator's business name, logo, photos, tour descriptions, and contact information as part of the Operator Listing for the duration of this Agreement.

**5.3 Widget License.** If KaiCast provides a co-branded widget, KaiCast grants Operator a limited, non-exclusive, non-transferable license to embed such widget on Operator's own website solely to display Condition Scores to Operator's customers. Operator may not sublicense, modify, or resell the widget.

## 8. FEES

**8.1 Base Partnership.** The base data partnership arrangement (Operator Listing, portal access, monthly aggregated report) is provided to Operator at **no charge** during the Term, in exchange for Operator's regular data contributions.

**8.2 Premium Features.** Additional features (e.g., priority placement, enhanced analytics, booking integrations) may be offered at additional fees, as set forth in a separate Order Form.

**8.3 Data Contribution Minimums.** If Operator submits fewer than [X] condition reports per month for [X] consecutive months, KaiCast reserves the right to downgrade Operator's listing to a basic directory listing or remove it, with 30 days' written notice.

## 9. TERM AND TERMINATION

**9.1 Term.** This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues on a month-to-month basis unless terminated.

**9.2 Termination for Convenience.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement for any reason with **30 days' written notice**.

**9.3 Termination for Cause.** KaiCast may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if Operator: (a) submits fabricated or materially inaccurate data; (b) is found to be operating without required licenses or permits; (c) violates applicable law in connection with its tour operations; or (d) materially breaches this Agreement.

**9.4 Effect of Termination.** Upon termination: (a) Operator's Listing will be removed from the Platform within 30 days; (b) Operator's access to the platform and co-branded widget is terminated; and (c) KaiCast retains the perpetual license to Operator-Submitted Data previously submitted.
